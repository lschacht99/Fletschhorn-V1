# Content strategy

The website now positions Waldhotel Fletschhorn as a private alpine chalet / private hotel buyout in Saas-Fee: a historic mountain property reserved for families, groups, weddings, retreats, ski weeks and private events.

The seven-page structure is: Home, The Property, Suites & Sleeping, Weddings & Private Events, Retreats & Groups, Dining/Wine/Hospitality, and Saas-Fee & Inquiry. Copy avoids room-by-room booking language and keeps every CTA inquiry-first.
