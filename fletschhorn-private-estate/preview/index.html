Why Saas-Fee

A car-free village surrounded by 18 four-thousand-meter peaks, known for winter skiing, summer glacier skiing and high-altitude mountain experiences.

Mountain experiences

Night sledging, the Ice Pavilion, Metro Alpin, marmots at Spielboden, the Feeblitz mountain coaster, hiking, rope park, Trotti scooters and SaastalCard summer benefits.

How to arrive

Arrive into the Saas valley, continue to the car-free village rhythm, and coordinate final arrival details with the private proposal.
